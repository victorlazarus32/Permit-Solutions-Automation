What Is Included For Each Permit

Each permit is worked individually. The county cannot pull older permits by expiration date, so every one is chased by its application reference. For each permit we:

  • Pull the full permit record and original application from Miami-Dade County archives, including the older files held on microfilm that must be requested and retrieved by hand.
  • Review the file to pinpoint exactly what is open or missing, such as a final inspection that was never completed, an expired application, or a required revision.
  • Meet in person with the correct trade department (Building, Electrical, or Mechanical). Each trade is a separate department and desk, so a single property can require several visits.
  • Confirm the close-out path with that department: renewal and inspection, a revision, a recertification, or close-out by affidavit, then prepare and submit everything they require.
  • Schedule and meet the inspector for any required inspection.
  • Track each permit to a final, closed status, confirm it is cleared in the county system, and provide you written confirmation.
Scope Limitations and Unknown Conditions

This proposal is based on the permit information currently available for the {{ 'properties' if properties|length > 1 else 'property' }} listed above. Additional county requirements may be discovered only after the original permit records are pulled and reviewed.

If Miami-Dade County determines that additional work is required beyond administrative close-out, permit renewal, inspection coordination, or standard close-out processing, that work is outside the flat fee and will require separate written approval from Client. Examples of excluded work include, but are not limited to:

  • Engineering reports
  • Affidavits
  • As-built drawings
  • New plans or revisions
  • Contractor repair work
  • Replacement work
  • Electrical, mechanical, building, or fire alarm corrections
  • Unsafe structure issues
  • New permits
  • Work required because of inspection failure
  • Work required because the original permitted condition no longer exists

Licensed Trade Work Not Included. Permit Solutions Services does not perform licensed electrical, mechanical, fire alarm, building, or repair work under this Agreement. If any licensed trade work is required, Client must hire the appropriate licensed contractor under a separate agreement.
